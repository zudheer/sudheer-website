Librarian Assist

A complete library management app for Kerala's libraries — books, members, lending, events, and finances, with offline-first storage and optional Google Drive backup.

Get it on Google Play com.sudo.librarian_assist

Books & Members

Manage your full catalogue and member directory, with bulk Excel import in the standard KSLC data-entry format — including automatic column matching if your sheet's headers don't line up exactly.

In-Out Register

Track lending and returns with serial numbers, call numbers, and member details — with month-by-month browsing and Excel export for your physical register.

Calendar & Holidays

Mark weekly and one-off holidays — lending automatically pauses on closed days, and test/demo data generation skips them too.

Events

Log library events with photos, contests, sub-events, and participant lists, and export a complete write-up to Word with your library's letterhead.

Finance & Fees

Record income, expenses, and membership fee collections, all tied back into your library's books.

Backup & Restore

Works fully offline by default. Optionally sign in with Google to back up and restore your data via your own Google Drive.
