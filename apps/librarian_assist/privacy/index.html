Privacy Policy

Librarian Assist — Last updated: 27 June 2026

Librarian Assist ("the App", "we", "us") is a library management app for librarians and library staff — managing books, members, lending records, events, and finances. This page explains what information the App collects, how it's used, and the choices you have. It applies to the Android (and, if released, iOS) versions of the App, published by Sudheer K.

In short: your library data (books, members, lending records, finances) is stored only on your own device, in a local database, unless you choose to back it up to your own Google Drive. The App shows ads (via Google AdMob) and offers optional Google Sign-In for Drive backup. We don't run our own servers and don't sell your data to anyone.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Library data you enter

Everything you type into the App — book records, member records, lending/return history, events, income & expense entries, membership fees, holiday calendar entries, and your library's profile (name, logo, address, contact number) — is stored locally on your device in a private database. We do not receive or have access to this data unless you explicitly export or back it up (see below).

1.2 Google Account information (optional)

If you choose to sign in with Google (used only for the Drive backup feature), the App receives your Google account's email address and basic profile info (name, profile photo) from Google, to show who's signed in and to authorize Drive access. We request only the drive.file permission scope — the App can only see and manage files it itself creates in your Drive; it cannot browse or access your other Drive files.

1.3 Backup data sent to your Google Drive (optional)

If you use the backup feature, the App uploads a copy of your local database and/or Excel exports to a folder it creates in your own Google Drive — not to any server we operate. This backup contains the same library data described in 1.1. You can delete these files at any time directly from your Google Drive.

1.4 Advertising data

The App shows banner ads via Google AdMob (Google Mobile Ads SDK). To serve and measure ads, Google's SDK may collect device and advertising identifiers, IP address, app activity, and approximate location, and may use this data to show personalized ads where you've consented to that. On launch, the App asks for your consent choices (required for users in the EEA, UK, and similar regions) via Google's User Messaging Platform before loading any ads that use personal data. See How Google uses information from sites or apps that use its services.

1.5 This website

This page (and the rest of sudheer.su-do.in) uses Google Analytics to understand visits to the site itself — this is separate from, and unrelated to, the App's own data practices described above.

2. How We Use This Information

  • To provide the App's core features — storing and displaying your library's records on your device.
  • To let you back up and restore your data via your own Google account, if you choose to.
  • To show ads that help keep the App free, and to measure ad performance.

We do not use your library data for advertising, and we do not sell any data to third parties.

4. Data About Your Library's Members (Patrons)

If you use the App to manage a library, the "Members" records you enter (names, contact details, etc. of your library's patrons) are personal data about third parties, entered and controlled by you or your library/organization. We act only as the provider of the local storage and optional backup tooling — your library/organization is responsible for having a lawful basis to store this data and for complying with any applicable data protection obligations toward your patrons (for example, informing them this data is kept and how to request its deletion).

5. Your Choices

  • Don't sign in with Google — every feature except Drive backup works fully offline, with no Google account.
  • Sign out at any time from the Backup & Sync screen; this revokes the App's access to your Drive.
  • Delete your data by uninstalling the App (removes the local database) and/or deleting the backup folder from your Google Drive.
  • Manage ad consent/personalization via the consent prompt shown on first launch.

6. Data Retention

Local data persists on your device until you delete it or uninstall the App. Backup files persist in your Google Drive until you delete them — we have no independent copy and cannot delete them on your behalf.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is a professional tool for library staff and is not directed at children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Security

Your library data is stored in your device's app-private storage, which other apps cannot normally access. Backup uploads to Google Drive use Google's standard authenticated, encrypted connections.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as the App changes. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above.
